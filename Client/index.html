The Problem

Pricing custom laser jobs can be challenging whether you are new or even experienced in the industry.

Getting job files from clients, running them through your lasers time calculator, and calculating qty breaks all have to happen before getting a quote back.

It's also easy to miss extra items like design time or handling time of the pieces between engraving. This can lead to lower profit jobs if unaccounted for.

This process can also take valuable time each day from you and even be a headache for your client as well.

What We Offer

We offer a simple yet comprehensive pricing application that allows you to bid custom jobs quickly and accurately.

With years of experience in the industry and suffering from the same pricing problems, we sat down to come up with a solution.

The days of emailing your client back hours later with a quote are behind you. Our goal was to create this with the idea of getting accurate prices back to your client while still on the inital phone call!

And this is currently free to use!
